MaltLabs is Now Backed by AWS: A New Chapter Begins

By Jésus Lopes April 12, 2025
AI AWS Automation

We’re proud to share some amazing news — MaltLabs is now officially part of the AWS Activate program!

This recognition marks an exciting step forward in our mission to empower small and medium-sized businesses with intelligent marketing automation and AI-driven customer experiences.

Being backed by AWS means more than just infrastructure credits. It’s about having access to one of the world’s most advanced cloud ecosystems — and using it to build smarter, faster, and more scalable solutions for our clients.

At MaltLabs, we’re committed to creating tools that simplify digital marketing for non-technical teams. With the support of AWS, we’re now able to accelerate the development of key features like:

  • AI-powered chatbots for WhatsApp and Instagram
  • Automated content generation using GPT-based models
  • Realtime analytics and campaign performance dashboards
  • And so much more…

This is only the beginning.

We’re grateful for the trust and support, and we’re excited to keep growing — not only as a company, but as part of a global community of builders, creators, and innovators.

Let’s build the future of marketing together — now, powered by AWS.
